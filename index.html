Project Components

Data Warehousing

For this segment of the project, our team developed a data mart that allows users to retrieve customer information based on parameters selected from the data mart form. To get the best results, we performed data selection, and data preprocessing before transferring the records to our database.

Data Mining

The second part of our project required us to perform the steps of knowledge discovery to gain insightful information contained within our data. We prepared several questions that we hoped to answer from the evaluation of our data set and gave a thorough presentation on our topic to our class.

Tools / Software used

  • R / R Studio
  • Tableau
  • MS Excel
  • Naive Bayesian Classification

Knowledge Gained

  • Hands-on experience with the process of knowledge discovery
  • Experience with data analysis and visualization tools
  • The importance of data preprocessing
  • Deeper understanding of classification algorithms
